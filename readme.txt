intrxn.com Pay Checkout for WooCommerce. The best way to accept cryptocurrencies.

== Description ==

intrxn.com Pay Checkout is a payment gateway plugin for WooCommerce. The best way for merchants to accept crypto and be paid in fiat or crypto.

intrxn.com Pay Checkout is a feature of intrxn.com Pay, which utilises intrxn.com API as a high performing native blockchain solution. This enables the transaction flows between crypto users and merchants seamless, cost-efficient and secure.

Read more about **intrxn.com Pay** [here](https://intrxn.com/payment-gateway/).

Become a intrxn.com Pay merchant by signing up [here](dev.intrxn.com). 

= Key features =

* Accept cryptocurrency payments (including Bitcoin, Ether, Litecoin, Ripple and more) from intrxn.com App users and other wallets' users
* Get settled in fiat currency via bank transfer or various cryptocurrencies
* Price your goods in your local fiat currency

= Customer's user journey =

1. The customer checks out his or her shopping cart.
2. The customer chooses intrxn.com Pay as checkout method.
3. The customer sees a new window pop-up with a QR code. The QR code is embedded with the payment information and has to be scanned using the intrxn.com App within 5 minutes.
4. The customer chooses his or her preferred cryptocurrency to fulfil this payment. 
5. Once the transaction is approved by intrxn.com Pay, the customer will see a confirmation screen on intrxn.com App, and the fiat equivalent amount will be deposited to the merchant's balance, as seen in the *Merchant Dashboard*.

== Installation ==

= Requirements =

* [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/)
* intrxn.com Pay [merchant account](https://dev.intrxn.com)

= Plugin installation =

1. Sign up for a [intrxn.com Pay merchant account](https://dev.intrxn.com).
2. From your Wordpress admin panel, go to Plugins > Add New > Search plugins and search for **intrxn.com Pay Checkout for WooCommerce**. 
3. Select **intrxn.com Pay Checkout for WooCommerce** and click on **Install Now** and then on **Activate Plugin**
4. Go to your WooCommerce settings and click **intrxn.com Pay Checkout for WooCommerce** to configure the plugin.

* Refer to our [Setup Instructions](https://intrxn.com/payment-gateway/).
